# EmpowerHer - a women safety app
## Overview
EmpowerHer -Safety Application that is a smart mobile solution designed to provide immediate assistance during critical situations for women. Built using Flutter, the app combines emergency alert tools, real-time communication, and safety automation into a single, user-friendly interface.

The application enables users to record and send voice evidence, activate a loud siren, and share their live location instantly with trusted contacts. With one-tap emergency actions such as auto calling, SOS alerts, torch activation, and medical assistance, the app ensures that help can be reached within seconds.

A key highlight of the system is its Smart Detection Module, which includes microphone access and panic movement detection toggles, laying the foundation for future integration with wearable devices and health monitoring systems. Visual indicators for vital signs like heart rate and blood pressure enhance situational awareness during emergencies.

By focusing on speed, reliability, and accessibility, the Emergency / SOS Safety Application serves as an effective safety companion for women, travelers, and individuals in high-risk environments. Its modular design allows easy scalability for advanced features such as AI-based panic detection, automated alerts, and cloud-based evidence storage, making it suitable for real-world deployment and future expansion.

## Key features:
Emergency Voice Recording:
Tap “Send recorded voice note” to start recording audio.
Tap again to stop recording instantly.
Designed for hands-free, fast evidence capture in emergencies.

Siren Alert System:
One-tap activation of a high-volume emergency siren.
Tap again to stop the siren.
Visual highlighting when the siren is active.

Live Location Sharing:
Fetches real-time GPS coordinates.
Opens location directly in Google Maps for easy sharing.

Quick Emergency Actions:
Auto call trusted contacts.
SOS alert button for instant help.
Torch, medical assistance, and audio recorder shortcuts.

Smart Detection System:
Microphone and panic movement detection toggles.
Designed for future wearable (smartwatch) integration.
Displays simulated vitals like heart rate and blood pressure.
